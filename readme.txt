== Description ==



The PTC Pinterest Widget is an awesome tool for your Wordpress website, enabling you to post a feed directly from Pinterest on your pages! The Pinterest slider is easy to install, easy to use and provides users with an ample amount of benefits.

Take a look at the features and benefits sure to be appreciated with the PTC Pinterest Widget.


i) The PTC Pinterest Widget has a great design –all that is visible is a icon for Pinterest until the visitor hovers over the page. Once this is done the      slider widget will pop out and present your Pinterest feed.


ii) Easy to Use – Anyone who visits your site can easily view what’s going on at your Pinterest account, as well as leave your site to visit and follow you if     they would like. You can easily take social media to the next level with the Pinterest widget.


iii) Advertise – Rather than sending out an email or a text, or going through the process of changing your entire site, the PTC Pinterest Widget can be used     to make announcements, post pictures and news and much more. You can easily advertise your contest, sale, etc. for all to see on the PTC Pinterest Widget right on your homepage!


iv) Options – There are tons and tons of options available for your Pinterest pins with the PTC Pinterest Widget. You can easily customize the PTC Pinterest Widget to your specifications, which includes the width and the height of the slider on your page.


v) Spacious - there’s a lot covered in the PTC Pinterest Widget, but it won’t take up a lot of space on your site. The PTC Pinterest Widget shows as an     icon on the side of your page. It is only visible once a user hovers around this Pinterest icon.


vi) Display text as well as pictures – Some of the plug-ins for Wordpress make you choose one or the other…but not this Wordpress plugin called the PTC Pinterest Widget.


vii) Compatible –The Wordpress Pinterest Widget is compatible with all browsers, including Chrome, Safari, Firefox and Internet Explorer


viii) All Software Supported –The Pinterest Widget can be used with all versions of the Wordpress website.


ix) Entertaining – With the PTC Pinterest Widget on your website you can keep visitors there longer, enjoying what you are offering to them. Along with      your blogs and info, pictures and posts, visitors to your site can play around and see all of your pins and more.


x) Post More – With the Pinterest widget you can easily add new posts right from your website without ever having to log into Pinterest. This may not seem      like a big deal but it really does make your life much easier and enables you to do so much more with your social networking.
     The long list of features and benefits of the PTC Pinterest Widget make it a plug-in that you should not wait to add to your Wordpress website. It will     give your website the edge that it needs to be a great success and so much more. Don’t go without it a day longer.


== Installation ==



This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.



1. Install the wordpress plugin first



You can install it via Wordpress Installer or can download the file first then Upload it on /wp-content/plugins/ directory



2. Activate the plugin



3. You will see pinterest settings from right side of your wordpress menu.



Configuration Options:



Pinterest Username : Default is "pinterest" - You can change that with your pinterest username



Margin Top - Height & Width : Their you have to Put Numeric Number as per your need.



Image scale : Default is 120 - Change it with a numeric value you prefer.
